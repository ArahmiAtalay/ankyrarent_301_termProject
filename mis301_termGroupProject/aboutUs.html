Mission Statement

Ankyra Rent's mission is to serve and represent the franchised new car dealers of Turkey, their management and employees.

Goals

Our two main goals are:

  1. To promote and enhance the franchise system and effectively communicate dealer views and concerns to all branches of the manufacturers and the public,
  2. To strengthen the financial position of members as retailers through professional programs and services that improve the business skills of management personnel and the technical capabilities of non-management employees.
